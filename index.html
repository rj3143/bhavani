Homams (Fire Rituals)

Homas (also called yagnas or fire rituals) are sacred ceremonies where offerings to the sacred fire with mantras remove negative forces and invite blessings. Listed below are the detailed homams we perform.

Major Homams

  • Shashti Poorthi — Ceremony for 60th year completion, marking a transition to spiritual life.
  • Kukuta Homa — Invoking the divine rooster of Lord Muruga — removes negativity and empowers protection.
  • Lakshmi Kubera Homa — For financial wellbeing and abundance.
  • Lakshmi Narayana Homa — Combined wealth & protection homa.
  • Muruga Homa — For courage, overcoming opponents and protection.
